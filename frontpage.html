Achilles Tendon Rupture

An Achilles Tendon rupture occurs when the tissue between your heel and your calf tears. There are 2 types of rupture; partial or complete tear. You will struggle to walk or even be unable to walk, depending on the severity you will need surgery to repair the tear. Although it can occur at any age; it is more common for 30-50 year olds. The incidence of Achilles Tendon Ruptures is more frequent, this is believed to be connected to the increased participation in physical exercise from the ageing population.
