Achilles Tendon Rupture

Rehabilitation

Post Surgery: how to get back to normal life

  • Rest: It is important post injury to rest well to allow the body to recover, listen to your body, if it is hurting give it chance to rest
  • A boot similar to the one pictured or a cast will be used for up to 12 weeks to maintain the foots position and take the pressure off the joint.
  • Movement in the joint will reduce swelling, but will also improve strength of the ruptured achilles tendon, although it is important not to over-do anything.
