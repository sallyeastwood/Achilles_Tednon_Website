Achilles Tendon Rupture

Heel Drops

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder width apart on a step, and hold onto a chair or the wall for balance.
  2. Slowly lower the injured foot as low as possible.
  3. Hold for 5-10 seconds, and then return to a neutral foot poistion.
  4. Repeat this 10 times.
