Achilles Tendon Rupture

Towel Stretch

  1. Sit down on the ground, with both legs stretched out in front of you.
  2. Place a towel or belt across the ball of your foot and hold either end of the towel or belt.
  3. Relax the ankle and keep your leg straight.
  4. Gentally pull the towel towards you until you can feel the stretch in your calf.
  5. Hold this for 30 seconds and relax for 30 seconds, and repeat the action 3 times.
